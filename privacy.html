Privacy Policy for ArtHub

Last updated: September 30, 2025 · Website: arthub.world

This Privacy Policy explains how ArtHub World (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use ArtHub (the “Service”).

Contact: support@arthub.world

1) Information we collect

  • Information you provide — account/profile details, email, messages, and content you submit.
  • From Facebook/Instagram (if you connect) — only permissions needed for features you use (e.g. public_profile, email, Pages/Inbox access if you explicitly enable those flows).
  • Automatic data — device, browser, IP address, usage analytics and logs for security, debugging, and improvement.

2) How we use information

  • Provide and improve the Service and its features.
  • Personalize the experience and measure performance.
  • Communicate with you about updates, security, and support.
  • Comply with law and platform policies.

3) Sharing

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, security) under contracts that protect your data.
  • Legal compliance and safety, when required.
  • No selling of personal information.

4) Legal bases (where applicable)

  • Performance of a contract (to deliver the Service).
  • Legitimate interests (analytics, security, fraud prevention).
  • Consent (for optional features; you can withdraw in Facebook settings or in-app where available).

5) Cookies & tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies for core functionality, analytics, and to remember preferences. You can manage cookies in your browser settings.

6) Data retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes described or as required by law, then delete or anonymize it.

7) Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data, and to object or restrict certain processing. To exercise rights, email support@arthub.world.

You can also review/revoke Facebook permissions in your Facebook account settings.

8) Security

We implement administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data. No method is 100% secure.

9) Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or older minimum where applicable). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10) International transfers

Data may be processed outside your country. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., contractual clauses).

11) User Data Deletion

If you signed in with Facebook/Instagram, you can request deletion of your data as follows:

  1. Email support@arthub.world with subject Delete my Facebook/Instagram App data, including your Facebook/Instagram user ID or the email associated with your account.
  2. Or submit the form at https://legal.arthub.world/data-deletion.html once published.

We will delete associated data within 30 days unless retention is required by law, dispute resolution, or security.

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date reflects the latest changes.
