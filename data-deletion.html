How to Delete Your ArtHub Data

If you signed in with Facebook/Instagram, you can request deletion of your data at any time. Choose one of the options:

  1. Email support@arthub.world with the subject Delete my Facebook/Instagram App data and include your Facebook/Instagram user ID or the email associated with your account.
  2. Or use an in‑app/account deletion control if/when available.

We will delete associated data within 30 days (or sooner where required by law). Some records may be retained if necessary for legal obligations, dispute resolution, or security.

See our full Privacy Policy: https://legal.arthub.world/privacy.html
